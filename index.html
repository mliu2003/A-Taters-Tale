Greenhouse gas emissions per kilogram produced of popular produce items.

Potatoes produce about 0.46 kilogram of greenhouse gases per kilogram of potato produced, making it one of the lowest producers of greenhouse gases (relative to other food products).

Varieties of potatoes

Russet potatoes are by far the most popular variety of potatoes planted, with about 80,000 acres of farmland being used to plant russet potatoes.

Is it worth it?

Though many factors can affect the output, potatoes are one of the most efficient crops to plant, having a higher value than cost per acre grown.

Potato Truck

Potatoes are transported across the states by truck! There is a truck called the Big Idaho Potato Truck that carries a 4-ton potato across the country every year.

Where are potatoes produced in the US?

When it comes to potatoes, Idaho reigns supreme. Its production far surpasses that of any other state, with Washington at second, and the rest of the nation left in the dust.

Comparing Prices of Different Crops

Potatoes are incredibly cost efficient for consumers! Purely judging by price per pound, potato easily outclasses other common popular vegetables, like tomatoes, broccoli, and corn.

French fries are king.

When the potato finally arrives onto the plates of consumers, Americans find themselves enjoying the product in all shapes and sizes. French fries are the most popular, closely followed by mashed potatoes and baked potatoes.

Chips are among the most popular ways potatoes are enjoyed by consumers.

Based on sales (millions of USD), potato chip lovers around the world predominantly enjoy Lay's, followed by Ruffles, Pringles, and other more niche brands.

Compared to similar carbs, potatoes are an excellent source of nutrition.

While popular carbs can also serve as adequate sources of nutrition, potatoes are capable of providing similar or even higher amounts of vital macronutrients. As an added bonus, potatoes act as a great nutrient source while having lower calories than other carbs, making potatoes a great weight management food.
