Greenhouse gas emissions per kilogram produced of popular produce items.

Potatoes produce about 0.46 kilogram of greenhouse gases per kilogram of potato produced, making it one of the lowest producers of greenhouse gases (relative to other food products).

How to eat??

Americans love to eat potatoes! Although potatoes can be enjoyed in many ways, the most common are french fries, mashed potatoes, and baked potatoes.
